Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States continue.

Here are three of the country's five top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million people.
  2. The Women's March was a worldwide protest on January 21, 2017, the day after the inauguration of Donald Trump.
  3. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew about a million people in 1993.
